Under the hood

What we build with, and how it stays safe

Stack

We build with React, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion on the frontend, Three.js for anything visual, and Node.js underneath. Automations run on n8n, connected through Twilio and powered by Groq. We also build on WordPress and PHP for clients who need that instead.

Security

Every workflow is built with security in mind. Your data stays protected at every step, from the forms you fill out to the automations running behind the scenes.
