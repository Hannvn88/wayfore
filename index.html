II.

Safe to hand your business to

security
01

Your data stays yours

We keep your information in its own space. It is never mixed with other clients, and never used to train outside AI models.

02

Keys kept under lock

Access keys are stored in a secure vault, given only the access they need, and refreshed regularly. Nothing is written into the code or shared in plain text.

03

Privacy by default

Your information is encrypted while it travels and while it rests, with a clear policy for how it is handled. We never sell it, and you can ask us to delete it whenever you like.

04

Nothing runs wild

We check every input for anything harmful, limit how often requests can be made, and give every agent firm limits and an off switch, so it can never run out of control.
